About Us

the owner and founder

Choso Yoora

Meet Choso Yoora, a dynamic 28-year-old entrepreneur who brings the essence of Japanese coffee culture to the vibrant streets of the Philippines. Born with a passion for both coffee and cultural exchange, Choso embarked on a journey to blend the flavors of Japan with the warmth of Filipino hospitality. Inspired by his travels and experiences, he recognized an opportunity to introduce a new dimension of coffee appreciation to the bustling streets of Manila. With unwavering determination and a keen eye for detail, Choso meticulously crafted his vision, envisioning a coffee shop where patrons could experience the serene tranquility of a Japanese tea house amidst the lively atmosphere of the Philippines.
In his pursuit of excellence, Choso delved into the intricate nuances of Japanese coffee craftsmanship, mastering the art of pour-over brewing and perfecting the delicate balance of flavors. Infusing his creations with a touch of Filipino flair, he curated a menu that celebrates both local and imported ingredients, offering a unique fusion of flavors that captivates the senses. With each cup served, Choso invites customers to embark on a sensory journey that transcends geographical boundaries, fostering a community united by a shared love for exceptional coffee and cultural exchange.
Mission
To bring the authentic Japanese Coffee shop in the Philippines aims to blend the calmness of Japanese culture with the energy of Filipino warmth. We're dedicated to crafting delicious coffee and pastries in a serene space, where people can unwind and connect. By using local ingredients and supporting our community, we strive to create a welcoming environment for everyone to enjoy the beauty of Japanese coffee culture.
Vision
Become a beloved destination where the fusion of Japanese precision and Filipino hospitality creates unforgettable moments for our customers. We aspire to be more than just a place to grab a cup of coffee. We want to be a cultural hub where people can immerse themselves in the art of coffee-making, experience the tranquility of Japanese aesthetics, and forge meaningful connections with others. Through our commitment to quality, innovation, and community engagement, we aim to set a new standard for coffee culture in the Philippines, inspiring both locals and visitors alike to embrace the beauty of Japanese-inspired living.
